#  Business Insights 360 Dashboard

## 1. Business Insights 360 – Executive Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze business performance, sales trends, profitability, and operational KPIs through dynamic visualizations and data-driven insights.

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Business Insights 360 Dashboard provides a centralized analytical view of key business metrics across sales, finance, and operations. It helps stakeholders monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make strategic decisions using real-time insights and interactive reporting.


## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

•  Power BI Desktop – Main platform used for dashboard development and data visualization.
•  Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
•  DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Implemented for KPI calculations, measures, and dynamic reporting logic.
•  SQL & Snowflake – Used for querying, managing, and analyzing large-scale business datasets.
•  Data Modeling – Established relationships among multiple business tables for efficient cross-filtering and analysis.
•  File Format – .pbix for dashboard development and .png for dashboard previews/screenshots.


## 4. Data Source

Source: Internal business datasets containing sales, customer, operational, and financial information.

The dataset includes:

* Sales transactions and revenue details
* Product and customer performance metrics
* Regional and category-level KPIs
* Operational and profitability data
* Time-based business performance analysis


## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Organizations often struggle with scattered business data across multiple systems, making it difficult to track KPIs, monitor performance, and identify actionable insights quickly.

Business leaders need a centralized and interactive solution to:

* Track sales and profitability trends
* Monitor operational performance
* Analyze customer and product performance
* Support faster and smarter decision-making


### • Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive business intelligence solution that:

* Provides a 360-degree view of business performance
* Enables real-time KPI monitoring
* Supports strategic and operational decision-making
* Improves reporting efficiency through automation


### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals

### KPI Summary Cards

Displays key metrics such as:

* Total Revenue
* Profit Margin
* Sales Growth %
* Total Orders
* Customer Count

### Sales Trend Analysis

Line and area charts showcasing monthly and yearly sales performance trends to identify seasonality and growth patterns.

### Regional Performance Dashboard

Interactive map and bar charts comparing sales and profitability across regions and markets.

### Product Performance Analysis

Highlights top-performing and low-performing products/categories based on revenue and profit contribution.

### Customer Insights

Visualizes customer purchasing behavior, retention trends, and contribution to overall revenue.

### Profitability Analysis

Tracks profit margins across categories, regions, and business units to identify optimization opportunities.

### Dynamic Filters & Slicers

Allows users to interactively filter data by:

* Region
* Product Category
* Time Period
* Customer Segment
